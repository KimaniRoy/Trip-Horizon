Travel Tips for Kenya

Make the most of your Kenyan adventure with these insights!

Best Time to Visit

Plan your trip between July and October for the Great Migration or December to February for dry weather.

Visa & Vaccinations

Check visa requirements and get vaccinated for yellow fever before arrival.

Photography Tips

Bring a good zoom lens for safaris and respect local photography rules.

Local Language

Learn basic Swahili phrases like "Jambo" (Hello) to connect with locals.
